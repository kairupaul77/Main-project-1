Artwork and Wall Decor Gallery Walls: Create a personal touch with a well-curated gallery wall that displays family photos, artwork, or personal memorabilia. The arrangement of the pieces can create symmetry or an eclectic vibe, depending on your style. Mirrors: Mirrors can make small spaces feel larger and brighter. Pay attention to the frame style—whether it’s ornate, minimalist, or industrial, it should blend with the room’s overall aesthetic.

Color and Paint Details Accent Walls: Adding an accent wall with a bold color or patterned wallpaper can bring life to a room without overwhelming it. Ceiling Paint: Don’t forget the ceiling! A pop of color or a soft contrast on the ceiling can make the room feel unique and well-thought-out. Paint Finishes: Use matte finishes for a modern look or glossy finishes for a more dramatic, reflective surface. Also, consider two-tone walls with different shades or textures.

Plants and Greenery Indoor Plants: Even a small plant on a shelf or coffee table can add freshness and life to a room. Choose plants that complement your interior’s color scheme and lighting conditions. Planters: Opt for stylish planters that complement your decor, whether it’s ceramic pots, hanging baskets, or geometric metal stands.
